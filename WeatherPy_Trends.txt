WeatherPy Observable Trends:

Max Temp Vs Latitude: 
-The Northern and Southern Hemisphere Mirror each other. As Latitude increases for both hemispheres(adversly for SH), 
 temperatures start to decline.

Humidity Vs Latitude:
-Humidity percentages increase as Latitude increases for the Norther Hemisphere.
-The Southern Hemisphere seems to have a consistent humidity rate throughout Latitude ranges 
 with a slight decrease in percentages as you lose Latitude.

Cloudiness Vs Latitude:
-The Northern and Southern Hemisphere share a same theme that as you go lower in Latitude, 
 you will see a reduction in Cloudiness Percentages.
